Food Security NYC

Food insecurity comes from the lack of access to enough nutritionally adequate food for an active, healthy life for all members of a household. Food-insecure families may worry that food will run out before they have enough money to purchase more, eat less than they should, or be unable to afford to eat balanced meals.

Urban neighborhoods with pervasive unemployment and poverty, lead to a lack of access to adequate, nutritious food. The South Bronx, for example, has the highest rate of food insecurity in the country, 37% , compared with 16.6% for New York City as a whole.
